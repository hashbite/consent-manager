Welcome to Consent Manager ‚Äì an intuitive, React-based toolkit designed to seamlessly integrate external services into web applications, ensuring compliance with GDPR and various data privacy laws. Consent Manager is more than a tool; it's a commitment to ethical, user-centric web experiences. Our goal is to empower React developers to easily align with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar laws, fostering a more secure and privacy-respecting digital world.
